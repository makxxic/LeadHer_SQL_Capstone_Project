# 🔑 Key Findings – Orion Workforce Analytics

## 1. Workforce Distribution
Some departments have significantly higher employee counts, suggesting core operational focus areas.

## 2. Salary Insights
- Clear variation in average salaries across departments
- Indicates differences in role seniority and specialization

## 3. Salary Segmentation
Employees fall into three clear salary bands:
- Low (<5000)
- Medium (5000–10000)
- High (>10000)

## 4. High Earners
A subset of employees earns above the company average, representing high-value talent.

## 5. Workforce Gaps
Certain job roles currently have no employees assigned → potential hiring opportunities.

## 6. Geographic Cost Distribution
Salary expenditure is uneven across countries, indicating regional cost concentration.
